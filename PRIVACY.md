# Privacy Policy — Bulk WhatsApp Sender

**Last updated:** March 12, 2026

## Overview

Bulk WhatsApp Sender is a free, open-source browser extension that helps users send WhatsApp messages in bulk via WhatsApp Web. This extension does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with any server or third party.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- No personally identifiable information
- No phone numbers, messages, or contacts
- No browsing history or web activity
- No analytics, telemetry, or tracking
- No cookies or fingerprinting

## Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) solely to save:

- Message templates you create
- Sending history logs

This data **never leaves your browser** and is only accessible to you. It can be cleared at any time by removing the extension.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|---|---|
| `activeTab` | To identify the active WhatsApp Web tab |
| `tabs` | To communicate with the WhatsApp Web tab |
| `scripting` | To inject the automation script into WhatsApp Web |
| `storage` | To save your templates and history locally |
| `host_permissions` (web.whatsapp.com) | To operate on WhatsApp Web — no other sites are accessed |

## Third-Party Services

This extension does **not** use any third-party services, APIs, analytics tools, or external servers.
